Where are we running?

Apr 30 2005

So some long two weeks ago I was in Boston with Steve. Our apartment hunting trip went very well and we decided on an apartment complex called The Legacy in Arlington. If you look here you can see that we are fairly close to the city. The nearest T stop (metro for you DCers) is a quick bike ride away and there is a bus stop right outside our apartment.

I have a few of the pictures I took that weekend here. There are few shots of the complex, a field and pond only about a minute away, and what (downtownish) Arlington looks like right outside of our complex.

In other news, I got to go to my first concert. Pete was the nice guy who got me the tickets to Lenny Kravitz (somewhat spontaneously, but kind of like a graduation/birthday/farewell present). I was apprehensive at first and was worried that I wouldn't be much of the concert type. Thankfully it was really very enjoyable. I bit loud for my tastes, but I got quite into it when he did Fly Away, American Woman, and Are you Going to Go my Way Are you Gonna Go My Way. Glad that I got the chance to go, I owe you one Pete
