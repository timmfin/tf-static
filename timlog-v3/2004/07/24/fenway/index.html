Fenway

Jul 24 2004
I thing that I knew I had to do while I was in Boston was to go to Fenway park (Red Socks baseball stadium for you on-sports fans). Fortunately, I got the chance to go to a game through work. I might be very busy, but who can turn down a free ballgame ticket? The socks were playing the Baltimore Orioles, my hometown team. I haven't been following the O's for some time, so I didn't wear the O's orange and black. But when we started winning, I made sure to give the Boston native interns a hard time :).
