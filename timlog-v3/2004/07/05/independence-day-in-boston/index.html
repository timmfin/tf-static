Independence Day in Boston

Jul 05 2004
I haven't been excited for Independence Day since I was a kid, but people in Boston are quite different. I first suspected something when I heard people talking about July 4th more than a month and a half early. Everyone was getting excited and for good reason. Yesterday (actually the whole weekend) there were tons of things going on. Free concerts (Boston Pops), events, patriotic sing-a-longs, and of course fireworks. Wow, those were darn impressive fireworks. Its been a while since I've seen serious fireworks (last time was 3-4 years ago in DC), so I don't know if it mostly because of improvements in firework technology or if Boston's show if just 10 times better than anyone else's. I guess that hundreds of thousands of people waiting for hours to see a 30 min show, can't be wrong. I'll see if any of the pictures I took with my shiny new digital camera came out.
